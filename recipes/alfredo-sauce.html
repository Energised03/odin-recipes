Alfredo Sauce

Great Alfredo sauce recipe that will always come out rich, and creamy! A deluctable sauce that instantly enhances boring old pasta, of plains vegetables. This is an Americanized Alfredo sauce that will certainly annoy Italians.

Ingredients

  • Stick of Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • Garlic, thinly sliced
  • Fresh Parsley, roughly chooped

Directions

  1. Add butter into a pan on low-medium heat; Stir in the heavy cream, and simmer
  2. Whisk the parmesan cheese, and garlic until heated through and smooth
  3. Stir in the parsley; Its ready to serve
