Spaghetti Sauce

This quick and easy todo spaghetti sauce is an instant classic. It takes only 3 ingredients to make while being one of the best tasting sauces. It takes only tomatoes, onion, and butter. This sauce creates the base for many other italian pasta sauce. Add a bit of basil, and parmesan cheese, and you create one of the tried and true pasta sauce thats going to impress everyone, even italian granmas.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of peeled tomatoes
  • 1 whole peeled onion
  • 5 tablespoons of butter

Directions

  1. Add the tomatoes into a bowl; Crush the tomatoes
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, onion, and butter into a large pan over medium heat.
    3. Stif the mixture gently, until it nearing a boil.
  3. Turn down the heat to Low-Medium; Simmer till the onions softens
  4. Discard the onion; Add Salt and Pepper to taste; Now you have a simple spaghetti sauce.
